# About Hero Section Images

## Required Image

Place the hero background image in this folder with the following specifications:

### Filename
- `about-hero-bg.jpg`

### Image Specifications
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD) or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or wider
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (ideally under 500KB)
- **Quality**: High-resolution, emotionally engaging

### Content Guidelines
The image should feature:
- Children at Sevashram in natural settings (classroom, playground, or daily activities)
- Founders (Shri Suresh Rajhans and Mayuri) interacting with children
- Natural lighting preferred
- Warm, uplifting atmosphere
- Clear focus on faces showing care, growth, and hope

### Technical Notes
- The image will have a deep blue overlay: `rgba(30, 58, 138, 0.55)`
- White text will be displayed on top
- Image will have a subtle zoom animation
- Ensure important subjects are centered for better composition with text overlay

### Example Scenarios
✅ Children studying together with natural smiles
✅ Founders with children in a caring moment
✅ Group photo showing the Sevashram community
✅ Daily life moments showing warmth and compassion

❌ Dark or poorly lit images
❌ Cluttered backgrounds
❌ Images with existing text overlays
