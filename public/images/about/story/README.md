# Our Story Section Images

## Required Image

Place the founder story image in this folder with the following specifications:

### Filename
- `founder-story.jpg`

## Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 800x600px (Desktop), optimized for 1000x750px
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 3:2 preferred
- **Format**: JPG or PNG (JPG recommended for photos)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (ideally under 400KB)
- **Quality**: High-resolution, clear focus

### Display Dimensions
- **Desktop**: 450px height (width auto, maintains aspect ratio)
- **Mobile**: 250px height (full width)
- **Border**: 2px amber border (#F9A826)
- **Corner Radius**: 16px rounded corners
- **Shadow**: Soft drop shadow for depth

## Content Guidelines

### Primary Subjects
The image should feature one or more of the following:

✅ **Founders with Children**
- Shri Suresh Rajhans and/or Mayuri with Sevashram children
- Natural, candid moments showing care and connection
- Warm, authentic interactions

✅ **Early Days of Sevashram**
- Historical photos from 2011 or early years
- The original tin shed (13x25 ft) if available
- Early group photos showing humble beginnings

✅ **Founder Portrait**
- Professional or semi-formal photo of the founders
- Warm, approachable expression
- Natural lighting preferred

### Composition Guidelines
- **Focus**: Clear focus on faces and expressions
- **Lighting**: Natural light preferred, warm tones
- **Background**: Sevashram campus or relevant setting
- **Mood**: Warm, hopeful, compassionate, trustworthy
- **Expression**: Genuine smiles, caring gestures

### What to Avoid
❌ Dark or poorly lit images
❌ Blurry or out-of-focus shots
❌ Cluttered or distracting backgrounds
❌ Images with existing text overlays
❌ Low-resolution or pixelated photos
❌ Overly formal or staged poses

## Visual Style

### Color Tone
- Warm and inviting

The image should convey:
